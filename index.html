How to Play

Objective

Match patterns on the board to your goal cards to score points. Collect as many goals as possible before your action cards run out.

The Board

The board has 10 spaces arranged in two rows of five. Duck items fill the board in a snake pattern — left to right on the top row, right to left on the bottom. Each duck is one of four colors: teal, purple, gold, or gray.

Goal Cards

Each goal card shows a color pattern. Tap a goal card to see its full description. When the board contains a matching arrangement, tap the card again to collect it and score its points. New cards are drawn automatically.

A gold glow on a card means the current board already matches that goal — tap it to collect!

You must complete or cancel any active action before collecting a goal.

Action Cards

Your hand of action cards lets you rearrange the board. Tap an action to see what it does and activate it. After completing or canceling an action, you return to the card list. Each use spends one card from your hand.

When you successfully match a goal, you draw two new action cards as a reward.

Actions

Teleport
Move any duck to any other occupied space. The displaced duck swaps to the vacated spot.
Swap
Swap two adjacent ducks (left, right, above, or below).
Gravity Assist
Make two adjacent swaps in a row. The second swap must involve one duck from the first.
Abducktion
Remove one duck from the board. The board refills from staging.
Dubabducktion
Remove two adjacent ducks. Both return to staging and the board refills.
Black Hole
Remove one duck and every other duck on the board that shares its color.
Parallel Universe
Swap any duck with the one directly above or below it, as many times as you like. Tap End Action when done.
Shape Shifter
Swap two ducks in the same row (adjacent or skipping one). Those two plus a third neighbor lock as your set to freely rearrange. Tap End Action when done.
Orbit
Select the upper-left duck of any fully occupied 2×2 block and rotate all four clockwise as many times as you like. Tap End Action when done.
Wormhole
Select a duck, then choose a new color for it from the palette.
Body Snatcher
Select three ducks to remove. They are recolored and you drag them back to any empty spaces.
Mass Abducktion
Always available. Discard exactly 3 action cards from your hand to clear the entire board and refill it.

End of Game

The game ends when all action cards in your hand are exhausted. Any goals that match at that moment are collected automatically. Your final score determines your title:
